IRON MAN


Iron Man is a superhero who wears a special suit made of metal that gives him amazing powers.
His real name is Tony Stark, and he is a genius inventor and businessman.
Iron Man can fly through the air, shoot powerful energy beams from his hands, and has super strength.
He uses his incredible abilities to fight against villains who want to harm innocent people.
Iron Man is a good guy who always tries to do what's right and protect the world from danger
