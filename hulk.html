HULK


The Hulk is a big, strong, and green superhero! He's actually
Dr. Bruce Banner, but when he gets angry or upset, he turns into the Hulk.
The Hulk is super strong and can lift really heavy things, like cars and even buildings! He's almost invincible, which means he's really hard to hurt.
But sometimes, the Hulk gets a little out of control and can cause a lot of damage.
