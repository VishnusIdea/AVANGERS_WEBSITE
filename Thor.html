THOR


Thor is a superhero from Marvel Comics, who is known for his incredible strength and power.
He is a mighty warrior and the god of thunder in Norse mythology.
Thor is usually depicted with his trusty hammer, Mjolnir, which he can use to control lightning and summon storms.
He is a member of the Avengers, a team of superheroes who work together to save the world from villains and other threats.
